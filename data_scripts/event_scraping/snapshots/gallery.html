  • Soulstice lands in London - for all Londoner souls ready to connect through music and emotion. The iconic Gallery Club opens its doors to our international family for a night of rhythm, unity, and pure energy. The lineup features Dustin Phil, delivering his signature blend of deep grooves and emotional resonance, Alessio Cristiano, bringing melodic sophistication and refined energy, and our loyal residents, the Italoboys, embodying the unmistakable Soulstice spirit. Join us for an unforgettable night where beats meet souls. Be Where We Are. Tickets are LIMITED so be sure to book in advance.
