      Why does RA charge a fee?x
      The RA fee covers the cost of providing our ticketing service.

      We have labour and office costs from our developers, who build and maintain RA's website and our event discovery App, the RA Guide. We provide round-the-clock customer and promoter support and infrastructure to ensure you can buy tickets securely and speedily in over 50 different countries. We cover all of these costs with one small administrative fee per-ticket.

      It goes deeper than that, too. RA is an independent company dedicated to supporting the electronic music community. By buying your tickets through RA, you become a part of that mission.
